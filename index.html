About Me

So, you want to know about me? I'm a long term computer nerd with 30+ years of playing in and on computers. This includes everything from the days of the IBM/360 with punch card entry and no terminal access up to the current world of touch screen creativity. I've come a long way through this and finally I'm here trying to build websites for the world.

In addition, I fill my free time with music; mostly local indie musicians; sci-fi, and a bit of ukelele play. This covers most of what I have time for. Someday maybe I'll make the contact page work and you can send me questions.
